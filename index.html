DFC Individual/Personalized Appointments

If you’d like to get detailed questions about our two-year degree program answered in a one-on-one setting, consider scheduling a personalized visit. During your visit, you’ll have the option to meet with an admissions counselor on our St. Paul Campus. To schedule your visit, please call (651) 962-6154 or email admvisit@stthomas.edu.
